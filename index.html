About Us

Changing Places Inc began in Jacksonville with a simple mission: deliver high-quality logistics solutions with a customer-first mindset. Our team is built from experienced professionals across the moving, storage, and transportation industries who are dedicated to reliability, safety, and transparency. With our 24/7 dispatch capabilities, real-time tracking, and a network that spans the U.S., we’ve grown into a trusted name in logistics.

Our Services

  • Residential & Commercial Moving: Safe, professional relocation for homes and businesses.
  • Storage Solutions: Monitored, climate-controlled storage facilities for short and long-term needs.
  • Freight Logistics: Freight coordination with real-time visibility and DOT-certified coverage.
  • Packing & Unpacking: Expert handling and customized packing services to protect your assets.

Careers

We're expanding our team! Join Changing Places Inc and help us build world-class mobile apps for iOS and Android, as well as maintain and improve our backend infrastructure. We're looking for talented developers to help us innovate and support our growing platform.

  • Mobile App Developer (iOS - Mid-Level): Responsible for building and maintaining iOS applications. Strong experience with Swift and Objective-C, as well as knowledge of Apple’s design principles and interface guidelines.
  • Mobile App Developer (Android - Mid-Level): Responsible for building and maintaining Android applications. Proficiency in Kotlin and Java, with experience using Android SDKs and design guidelines.
  • Mobile App Developer (Senior - Full Stack): Lead development efforts for both iOS and Android mobile applications. Strong expertise in cross-platform frameworks (Flutter, React Native) and mobile architecture, as well as backend integration.
  • Backend Developer (Senior - Node.js/Java): Focus on building robust, scalable backend APIs and services. Strong experience with Node.js, Java, and databases such as MongoDB, PostgreSQL, or MySQL.
  • Full Stack Developer (Mid-Level): Responsible for both front-end and back-end development of mobile app features, including UI/UX and API integration. Strong knowledge of JavaScript, Node.js, and mobile app development frameworks.
  • DevOps Engineer (Senior): Build, automate, and maintain the deployment pipelines for our mobile apps and backend systems. Expertise in containerization (Docker, Kubernetes) and CI/CD pipelines.
  • Mobile QA Engineer (Mid-Level): Ensure the stability, reliability, and performance of our mobile applications. Experience in mobile automation testing for iOS and Android, using tools like Appium or Selenium.
  • Mobile App Developer (Junior): Entry-level role to assist in building and maintaining mobile apps for both platforms. Experience with basic mobile development technologies, eagerness to learn, and grow in a collaborative team environment.
  • Database Administrator (Mid-Level - SQL): Design and maintain relational databases to support mobile app backends. Strong proficiency in SQL and experience in optimizing database performance for mobile apps.
  • Product Manager (Mobile): Drive the roadmap for our mobile applications, working closely with development teams. Experience with mobile product development and UX/UI best practices to ensure user-centric solutions.
