About Us

Changing Places Inc began in Jacksonville with a simple mission: deliver high-quality logistics solutions with a customer-first mindset. Our team is built from experienced professionals across the moving, storage, and transportation industries who are dedicated to reliability, safety, and transparency. With our 24/7 dispatch capabilities, real-time tracking, and a network that spans the U.S., we’ve grown into a trusted name in logistics.

Our Services

  • Residential & Commercial Moving: Safe, professional relocation for homes and businesses.
  • Storage Solutions: Monitored, climate-controlled storage facilities for short and long-term needs.
  • Freight Logistics: Freight coordination with real-time visibility and DOT-certified coverage.
  • Packing & Unpacking: Expert handling and customized packing services to protect your assets.
